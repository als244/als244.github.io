About Me

Hi, I'm Andrew Sheinberg. I am a first-year PhD student studying Computer Science at Princeton University. Prior to Princeton, I lived in Salt Lake City for a year to focus on preparing for grad school; I was fortunate to work with Professor Abhishek Bhattacharjee's group at Yale during that time. Before that, I lived in Chicago where I worked as an options trader on an index volatility strategy desk. I attended Yale University where I studied Computer Science & Mathematics. I grew up in Barrington, RI. I am researching computer systems, with an emphasis on maximizing performance and resource utilization within clusters composed of heterogeneous hardware.
