About Me

Hi, I'm Andrew Sheinberg. I will be starting at Princeton University pursuing a Computer Science PhD. I spent the past year living in Utah where I prepared for grad school and worked with Professor Abhishek Bhattacharjee's group at Yale. Prior to Salt Lake, I lived in Chicago where I worked as an options trader on an index volatility strategy desk. Before that, I attended Yale University where I studied Computer Science & Mathematics. I grew up in Barrington, RI. I am researching computer systems, with an emphasis on maximizing performance and resource utilization within datacenters/supercomputers/clusters composed of heterogeneous hardware.
